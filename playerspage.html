    Carlos Alcaraz

    A young, talented player who has quickly risen to the top of the rankings, known for his aggressive baseline game and versatile style.

    Achievments

    Grand Slam Titles:

    • 2022 US Open
    • 2023 Wimbledon
    • 2024 Roland Garros
    • 2024 Wimbledon

    World No.1 Ranking: became the youngets player to reach the top of the ATP rankings in septemer 2022

    Masters 1000 Titles: Alcaraz has won six Masters 1000 titles, including the 2025 Monte Carlo Masters.

    Other Titles: He has won a total of 18 ATP Tour-level singles titles.

    Records

    • Youngest US Open winner since Pete Sampras in 1990
    • Youngest major champion since Rafael Nadal at the 2005 French Open.
    • Youngest ATP 500 champion.
    • First player to beat both Nadal and Djokovic in the same clay-court event

    Novak Djokovic
